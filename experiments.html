Exp. 1

  1. You guys will work in a group, so 4 people make up a group. It doesn't mean you cannot talk with other group's members, we are all part of roboclub family.
  2. Follow the tutorials of resources, and learn the basis of arduino by yourself, it's very easy to get started with arduino programming. BTW, you'd better download Arduino IDE before you go to lab.
  3. Check our calandar and pick a avaliable time to come to the lab with your team. But make sure that period hasn't been applied by other groups before. ( You can apply for using lab by contacting 张文轩 or 陈锐彬 on QQ, they will add your group to the calendar and other groups won't have access to the lab at that time )

Exp. 2

Bluetooth Beginner

  1. Learn how to set parameters of a bluetooth module by reading the tutorial in Lec3.zip. Then choose 2. or 3. to start your bluetooth learning.
  2. Implement the communication between two arduino boards. ( Any volunteers would wirte a tutorial of this experiment for our club? He or she will get a 'Bluetooth Module' as a reward. )
  3. Implement the communication between arduino board and your smart phone. You may need to download an app("蓝牙串口助手") that can convert bluetooth data to serial data.

Arduino Project

  1. Getting started with your arduino project as soon as possible.
  2. If you have any questions, create a new topic in Group Talk, that's the best way we learn together.
